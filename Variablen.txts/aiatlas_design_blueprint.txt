  ------------------------------------------
  21 — PIXEL‑LEVEL DESIGN RULES (12 RULES)
  ------------------------------------------

1.  Empty space should occupy at least 70% of the viewport.
2.  Primary focal object should never exceed ~5% of the canvas.
3.  Motion speeds should stay below 0.6x normal UI animation speed.
4.  Limit the palette to 3–4 core colors.
5.  Use glow and light gradients instead of sharp highlights.
6.  Every scene must have a single visual focal point.
7.  Text blocks should rarely exceed two lines.
8.  Maintain large vertical spacing between sections (200–400px).
9.  Background layers must move slower than foreground layers.
10. Interactive nodes should pulse subtly every 3–5 seconds.
11. Avoid rectangular UI boxes when possible — prefer floating elements.
12. Every scroll transition should visually feel like a camera movement.
